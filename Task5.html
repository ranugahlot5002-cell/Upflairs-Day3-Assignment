Fascinating Science & Math Facts

Chemistry Basics

One of the most essential molecules for life on Earth is Water.

The chemical formula for water is H2O, meaning it contains two hydrogen atoms and one oxygen atom.

Physics & Astronomy

Albert Einstein's famous equation for mass-energy equivalence is E = mc2.

The distance from the Earth to the Sun is approximately 1.496 × 108 kilometers.

Math Logic Symbols

In mathematics, we use various symbols to compare values:

  • Multiplication: 5 × 5 = 25
  • Division: 10 ÷ 2 = 5
  • Not Equal To: 10 ≠ 11
  • Less Than or Equal To: 4 ≤ 5
  • Greater Than or Equal To: 8 ≥ 8
